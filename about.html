About Drynetics

Where precision meets care since day one

Our Story

Founded in Bangalore with a passion for premium fabric care, Drynetics was born from the vision of bringing international-quality dry cleaning to North Bangalore. Starting with a single facility in Hunasamaranahalli, we have grown to serve thousands of customers across the city.

Our commitment to quality, eco-friendly practices, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do. Every garment that passes through our hands receives the same meticulous attention to detail, whether it is a delicate silk saree or everyday workwear.

Recently, we expanded to Bagalur to bring premium care closer to more communities. As we continue to grow, our founding principles remain the same: exceptional fabric care, sustainable practices, and a relentless focus on customer happiness.
