We recommend dry cleaning suits every 3-4 wears, or immediately if there are visible stains or odors. Regular brushing and proper hanging between wears can extend the time between cleanings.

For fresh stains, blot (don't rub) with a clean cloth. For oil stains, apply talcum powder. For coffee/tea, rinse with cold water immediately. For stubborn stains, it's best to bring the garment to us for professional treatment.

Clean all garments before storage. Use breathable garment bags (not plastic). Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Add cedar blocks or lavender sachets to repel moths.
